My name is Wu Yang. I woke up in a haze of confusion, my head pounding with pain. Just a moment ago, I was partying on a private yacht on Blue Star, but now I was cramped inside a wooden cage, my limbs bound tightly by rough vines. Outside, the air was thick with the scent of damp earth, roasting meat, and a strange, musky perfume. Women, tall and powerfully built, clad in untamed animal skins with spears of obsidian in their hands, walked in a circle around my cage, speaking in a low, rhythmic grunt that sounded like a primitive chant. The system in my head hummed: [MULTIRACIAL BLOODLINE SYSTEM INITIALIZED. TARGET HOST: WU YANG. LEVEL: 1. AVAILABLE BLOODLINES: HUMAN, BEASTMAN, SHADOW ELF, PRIMITIVE GIANTS.] I had just a second of shock before my eyes adjusted. Five tall women, their muscular shoulders adorned with bone necklaces and clay paint, stood watching me with broad, curious grin. They were members of the Silver-Hawk Tribe, an all-female clan that ruled the western forests. They looked at me not with fear, but the way a collector looks at a rare butterfly. Strong, young, and clean-shaven; I was a prize in their world. The woman at the front was built like a stone wall, her forearms scarred and her hair white with bone dust. Her weapon of black stone was braced in her hand. I opened my status pool: 12 units of energy. The bloodline fusion required 100. I needed a way out, or I’d be the main course in their spring sacrifice. My hand moved inside the cage, the wood rough against my palms. I reached for a sharp bone fragment on the cage floor. The leader, who I named Gara, saw me move. She didn't call her warriors. She walked closer. Her spear was pointed at my face. I looked Gara in the eyes, her gaze sharp, and I activated the first system action: Bloodline Scan. A red message appeared: [TARGET FOUND: GARA. LEVEL: 18. BLOODLINE: PRIMITIVE HUMAN/HAWK DUAL-GENE. STATUS: ENGINES DORMANT. ACQUISITION COST: 30 ENERGY units.] I had 12 energy units. But I had my own blood. I pressed my wrist against the sharp bone piece. System: [BLOOD ACQUIRED. ENERGY CAPACITY INFLated TO 150. CHOOSE BLOODLINE FOR FUSION.] I selected primitive giants. My body expanded. Not slowly; my arms became thick, my chest wide, and my height increased by 40 cm in 3 seconds. The vines around my wrists broke cleanly. The wooden cage cracked, then collapsed outward in a shower of splinters. Gara stepped back, her obsidian spear held high. She shouted a command to her warriors, and they formed a circle around me. I didn't reach for Gara. I reached for her spear. My fingers closed around the shaft of black stone, and I twisted. The spear broke in her hand. I pulled Gara to me, my hand holding her wrist with giant strength. The system message flashed: [FIRST CONTACT SUCCESSFUL. GARA FOLDED TO YOUR FORCE field. BLOODLINE MERGER BEGINNING. REWARD: 50 ENERGY UNITS.] Gara looked at me, her eyes wide, and her warriors lowered their spears. A spear hung on her back. One step at a time. The real show was about to begin.
